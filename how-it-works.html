Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to rent from neighbors?
Yes! We verify users (look for the Blue Tick) and encourage reviews. We recommend meeting in public spaces within the society for pickups.
How do payments work?
Currently, payments are settled directly between neighbors (via UPI or Cash) upon pickup. We are working on integrating in-app payments soon.
What if an item gets damaged?
Renters are responsible for returning items in the condition they received them. We recommend taking photos before and after rental.
