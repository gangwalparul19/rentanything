Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how RentAnything ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, list an item, request a rental, or communicate with other users.

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Verification Data: Government ID (for optional verification badges or high-value rentals), which is processed securely.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform, including search queries and interaction logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Facilitate rental transactions and communication between users.
  • Verify your identity and build trust within the community.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: When you enter a rental agreement, necessary contact details are shared with the other party to facilitate the transaction.
  • Service Providers: With vendors who perform services for us (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights/safety of RentAnything or others.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of our systems 100%.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rentanything.shop.
